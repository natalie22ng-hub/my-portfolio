Kebaya inspired Cardigan
crochet

As a Malaysian, the Baju Kebaya holds a special place in our cultural identity. Traditionally adorned with intricate floral patterns, I reimagined this classic garment in crochet, using large open weaves to ensure breathability—ideal for Malaysia’s tropical climate. I have since developed and published a pattern for this modern interpretation, which is now sold on international platforms, sharing the beauty of Southeast Asian culture with a global audience. To date, over 100 copies have been sold worldwide. You can find the pattern here.
